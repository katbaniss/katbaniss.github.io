Business relevance

Mobile service business

The mobile web provides users with a great opportunity to always be able to access information from any location. With the rapid adoption of mobile devices by consumers mobile commerce has become an attractive market for enterprises. This has triggered companies to introduce mobile versions, also called Mobile service businesses, that provide users with mobile company website versions.

Mobile service characteristics

Compared to access the internet via desktop browsers, the mobile web provides several advantages:


  1. Portability
    • Definition: Permanent access from mobile communication and information technology devices.
    • Purpose: Location-independent access to information.
  2. Personalization
    • Definition: Alignment to individual user experience design.
    • Purpose: Based on the collection and evaluation of accessed information, companies provide their user need-based information and products.
  3. Approachability
    • Definition: Possibility for users to reach out to the company/ organisation anytime.
    • Purpose: Enterprises interest to foster customer relationships.
  4. Localisability
    • Definition: Determination of the userâ€™s geographical position by means of triangulation within the active network cell or GPS.
    • Purpose: learning about consumer habits through provided context information including location, actions, time and interests.
  5. Availability
    • Definition: Immediate availability of information
    • Purpose: Access to information anywhere and anytime without time delays.
